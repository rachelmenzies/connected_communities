Get Involved

Connected Communities is building local partnerships to strengthen digital confidence and access across Dundee. Whether you run a service or want to volunteer your time, we would love to hear from you.

For Services And Charities

If your organisation supports people with digital skills, employability, community learning, or inclusion, we are keen to collaborate. We can work together to share learning, co-design practical resources, and connect people to the right local support.

You may want to get involved if you:

  • run a frontline service, charity, or community programme in Dundee
  • support people who face barriers to digital participation
  • want to shape tools and guidance that reflect real local needs

For Volunteers

We also welcome volunteers who want to support communities in Dundee with everyday digital skills. You do not need to be a technical expert, just patient, encouraging, and interested in helping others build confidence.

You could help by:

  • supporting informal digital learning in community settings
  • helping people practice tasks such as online forms, email, or video calls
  • sharing your time and experience in a friendly and inclusive way

Contact Us

If you would like to partner with us or volunteer, please email Rachel at r.menzies@dundee.ac.uk. We are happy to arrange a conversation to explore what involvement could look like for you or your organisation.
